About The Club

The ALIET Techpreneur Club (ATC) is a student-led initiative to foster innovation and entrepreneurship among the student community. Our mission is to:

  • Provide a platform for tech innovation
  • Connect students with industry mentors
  • Develop entrepreneurial skills
  • Support student startups

This inaugural event marks the beginning of an exciting journey towards creating the next generation of tech entrepreneurs.
